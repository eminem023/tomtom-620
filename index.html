How to Update TomTom GO 620 Maps

The TomTom GO 620 offers real-time traffic updates, voice control, and hands-free calling features, making it a smart companion for navigation. To keep it functioning optimally, regular map updates are essential. Here's a step-by-step guide to updating your device maps and firmware.

Why Update Your GO 620?

  • Stay up to date with new roads, routes, and speed limits
  • Ensure improved routing and accurate ETAs
  • Receive software enhancements and security patches
  • Access new features released by TomTom

Update Process Using Wi-Fi

  • Power on your TomTom GO 620
  • Connect to a secure Wi-Fi network
  • Tap Menu > Settings > Updates & New Items
  • The device will check for map and system updates
  • Select available updates and tap Update Now
  • Wait for the download and installation to complete

Need a Computer?

  • If Wi-Fi is unavailable, use MyDrive Connect on a PC or Mac
  • Connect the GO 620 to your computer via USB
  • Log in to your TomTom account on the software
  • Download and install the latest updates

Tips for a Smooth Update

  • Use a fast Wi-Fi connection for quicker downloads
  • Keep your device plugged into power

Troubleshooting Common Issues

  • Update fails: Restart your device and retry
  • Wi-Fi not connecting: Check your network or restart the router
  • Insufficient storage: Insert a microSD card

Updating your TomTom GO 620 ensures you always have the latest navigation data at your fingertips for a smoother and safer journey.
